ShareAscan gives you more control, more visibility, and more meaningful connection in the real world. Our patent-pending wearable QR technology gives you the power to turn your clothing and everyday items into instant digital connection points. With a simple scan, people can access the content you choose—your videos, your photos, your updates, your message. You decide what they see and when they see it.

This is a new kind of social media—one that finally bridges the virtual world with the actual one. Instead of waiting for people to search for you online, your presence becomes immediate and effortless. Whether you’re building a brand, promoting your work, networking, performing, selling, or simply expressing yourself, your scan becomes your voice. And with our app built around privacy and control, you can update your content anytime, block unwanted viewers, or open your world publicly—all on your terms.

What Makes ShareAscan Different

  • Your Message Can Change Anytime Update your photo, video, link, or note whenever you want — your QR code never changes.
  • Products As Unique As You Are Every item is custom-made with your personal QR code. No two codes are ever the same.
  • Instant Connection Your scan can link to a memory, a message, a profile, a shop, a playlist, a cause… anything you want to share.
  • Built For Everyone Use ShareAscan to share your thoughts, tell people how you feel right then, promote your content, surprise someone with a personal message, or add digital interactivity to events and merchandise.
  • You’re In Control Your content is private, editable, and secure. You decide what others see, who can see it, and who can interact with you.
